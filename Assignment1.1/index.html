  • High Concept:

    Hit the ball with the club.

  • Theme:

    Golfer

  • Mandated Variety:

    When you hit the ball there is a random number generator that picks 1 out of the 4 golf hit sounds

  • Prototype Goal:

    The goal is to get a feel for the controls and how the game sounds when you hit the ball

  • Player Experience Goals:

    I want players to experiance hitting the ball with the club.

  • Gameplay:

    The player can move the club up, down, left, or right with wasd. Also with q and e, the player can then rotate the club left or right. The system will play a golf hit sound when the club hits the ball. Your job is to try and get the ball in the top right and left light green areas without letting the ball hit the bottom. There is nothing stoping the player from having the ball go to the bottom light green area and no scoring system when you get the ball in the top right and left light green area. After that you would score then the ball would reset, but not your club.

  • Strategies:

    The strategy is to hit the ball. That is all for now.

  • Story/Setting/Premise:

    The game is set in a random world I made in Tiled, and your character is the golf club. Your golf club is forever traped in here with only one ball to hit until the player chooses to close the browser. The player is the only one that can make the golf club have a purpose in moving the club. It won't have any other life besides hitting this single ball in this browser. This game is purely abstract because I wanted to use one of theme cards some way.
