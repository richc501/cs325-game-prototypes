  • Story/Setting/Premise:

    The game is set in a distant future and your a space ship fighter from the Fighter Stargazer Squadron. You are the sole suvivour from a past battle you must kill all the aliens to get revenge for your fellow dead comrades. The aliens shoot completely random at you and moves randomly. Killing the aliens increases your score. The red circles are stars that you collect to increase your score, and are palced around the map at random. This game really has no backstory just the first thing I thought of when I heard stargazer. The tension bewteen the space ship and aliens will make the gameplay more addicting to keep trying to beat the game.
