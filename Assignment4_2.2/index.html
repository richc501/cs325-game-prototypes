  • High Concept:

    You are a chicken and you must collect all your eggs around the map and then get to the other side to win the game.

  • Theme:

    Chicken

  • Mandated Variety:

    I used a platformer style with sprites falling from the sky that can hurt the player. With a random number genterator determing the which x position it'll spawn at. I also added water to be water in the caves and have the water randomly switch from being water to being peanut oil that frys off one health point

  • Prototype Goal:

    My mechanic I was polishing the wall jumping, getting to the other side to win while testing a health and lives system.

  • Player Experience Goals:

    I want my players to feel like they are a chicken trying to get all the eggs and get to the other side while feeling like they will die trying.

  • Gameplay:

    W is jump, A and D are left and right. To wall jump left or right on a block HOLD W + D/A and the let go of D/A when you touch the block. To wall jump forward left or right on a block HOLD W + HOLD A/D. The player can wall jump left or right with a wall next to the player. The player can use the wall jump mechanic to climb up a wall and fling themselves the direction they are facing. The player goal is to find and collect all the collectable eggs before the player dies. Mean while there are cleavers that damage the player that fall from the sky and stay on the ground trying to kill the player and exploding soda bottles also falling from the sky. The cleavers do despawn after twelve seconds. However, the exploding soda bottles does not hurt the player, but cause some of the cleavers to be removed. Only the cleavers or the player can cause the soda bottles to explode. If the player is caught in the explosion, then the player is forced in the air by the explosion. The water will randomly transform to boiling peanut oil. So when the chicken trys to go take a swim the peanut oil causes the player to lose one health point per one second the player is in the peanut oil.

  • Strategies:

    The best strategy is to go backwards early and move quickly since cleavers spawn every second (but now despawn after twelve seconds) and soda bottles spawns every two and a half seconds around your sprite.

  • Story/Setting/Premise:

    My game is set in a pocket demenion where the god of this demenion is a mad chef that wants his chicken dinner now. The sole chicken doesn't want to become his chicken dinner, but has lost all his eggs. This chicken must find all his eggs and get to the other side to save himself from the mad chef. This mad chef is enraged and starts throwing cleavers to hurt this poor chicken and even soda bottles that explode on imact from the chicken or the cleavers. The chef's isanity has increased and now is turning the water in the caves into hot peanut oil in hopes have a fried chicken dinner. This causes the chicken to have mental break downs, but overall he just want to get to the other side without becoming chicken dinner.

  • Target Audience:

    My target audience would be people that love platformers.

  • Play Time:

    The game takes about two to three minutes to complete if you know where all the eggs are. But if you dont it may take about two extra minutes to back track through the map. The game really depends on your skill level and how lucky you get with the RNG not killing your chicken.
