  • High Concept:

    You are a chicken and you must collect all your eggs around the map and then get to the other side to win the game.

  • Theme:

    Chicken

  • Mandated Variety:

    I used a platformer style with sprites falling from the sky. With a random number genterator determing the which x position it'll spawn at.

  • Prototype Goal:

    My mechanic I was evaluating was wall jumping and getting to the other side to win.

  • Player Experience Goals:

    I want my players to feel like they are a chicken trying to get all the eggs and get to the other side while feeling like they will die trying.

  • Gameplay:

    W is jump, A and D are left and right. To wall jump left or right on a block HOLD W + D/A and the let go of D/A when you touch the block. To wall jump forward left or right on a block HOLD W + HOLD A/D. The player can wall jump left or right with a wall next to the player. The player can use the wall jump mechanic to climb up a wall and fling themselves the direction they are facing. The player goal is to find and collect all the collectable eggs before the player dies. Mean while there are cleavers that damage the player that fall from the sky and stay on the ground trying to kill the player and exploding soda bottles also falling from the sky. However, the exploding soda bottles does not hurt the player, but cause some of the cleavers to be removed. Only the cleavers or the player can cause the soda bottles to explode. If the player is caught in the explosion, then the player is forced in the air by the explosion.
